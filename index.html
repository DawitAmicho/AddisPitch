Our Story

Born in the heart of Addis Ababa

We noticed that booking a football pitch in Addis involved too many phone calls, uncertain availability, and manual payments.

Built by football fans for football fans, AddisPitch brings 24/7 real-time booking and secure Telebirr integration to the local game.

Instant Access

No more "calling the guy." See real-time slots for pitches in Bole, CMC, and beyond.

Secure Payments

Pay securely with Telebirr or CBE Birr. Receive your entry QR code instantly.

Community First

We support local pitch owners and help organize community tournaments across the city.
